Welcome to the Leverj staking app. The LEV staking process requires two main actions:

  1. To approve LEV for being staked (this requires that you have LEV!)
  2. To stake the amount of LEV for the remainder of the current staking period (this requires approval from 1)

Below is a guide to help you through the steps on this app:

  • Step 1: Choose method
    In this step, you will choose whether you want to engage in staking automatically via MetaMask wallet, or to manually copy transaction parameters for approving and staking
  • Step 2: Get account information
    In this step, you provide the Ethereum address that contains your LEV that you want to stake. The app will grab the information on your account relevant for this purpose.
  • Step 3: Approve LEV in account
    In this step, you can choose to approve some of the LEV in your address to be staked in the current period. This will give you some transaction parameters that you need to copy into your wallet of choice. Please refer to blog.
  • Step 4: Stake Approved LEV
    In this step, you will then stake the amount of approved LEV on the account. This will provide you the data field and account needed to finalize the staking of your LEV in the contract. Please refer to blog.
  • Step 5: Confirmation
    Here you can see that the staking action from Step 4 has successfully been executed. This step will show the amount of LEV staked and give you a link to the relevant transaction(s) on the blockchain.
